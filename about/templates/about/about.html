About Us

Boardom Be Gone is an Irish business, we sell board games and card games for all ages and interests. We are a family run business and we are passionate about board games and card games. We have a number of different types of games, such as tabletop games, strategy games, family games and more. We have been in business for over 10 years and we have a lot of experience in the industry. We are always looking for new games to add to our collection, so if you have any suggestions, please let us know. We are always happy to help you find the perfect game for you.
